    Career as a Developer

    I have always been passionate about tech and IT related appliances and gadgets when growing up as a kid. As a programmer, My career officially started in the University of Bamenda. Unlike most computer programmers, I wrote my first program in first year in the University, at age 20. It didn't really make sense to me since it was the most popular computer program "Hello World!". This fact that I couldn't really understand the applications and need of printing out a text in a black and white screeen aroused so much curiosity in me. I sought to fully understand what that really means. My quest to fully understand this led me to a whole new world in my career. In the beginning, it sounded somewhat ackward to me as I didn't understand some concepts as a bignner. This led me to a quest of making new IT friends, drawing closer to my computer science lecturers and attending Tech related events like the Netsquard, GDG, WTM and more. At the end of my Bsc, in Industrial Chemistry, I was so passionate about Tech related fields. I did alot of research and considered enrolling in free online courses on programming. I equally considered applications of tech in Chemistry related fields as well. This was the point in me career, where I fully engaged in the tech world. I applied in on a scholarship scheme in Seven Advanced Academy and I was shortlisted as a student. This gave me even more energy and courage to study more. I have always looked at the challenges face by my community like; intergration of technology to solve daily challenges, Incoorperation of tech in the educational sector of my country, the use of tech in waste disposal and environmental education and more and I think this is a great step in my career to solve these challenges.
