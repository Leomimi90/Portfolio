    My Vision

    What do you think of when you look around and try to understand how the world around you works?. Have you often wondered why you or some people you know never seem to show pesistent progress in their personal life, Career and other aspect? Think about the youths! How do you bring out the best out of them, Make them live lifes that are Godly, responsible and productive. In Africa we often say the youths being leaders of tomorrow. But we clearly see this is not evident. We often wonder about asking what is the issue. Should we cut off all leaders, how do we bring up that generation that changes everything. I call it the mind revolution. Train up a child in the way he should grow and when he is old, he will not depart from it.

    Music Career

    "My Music Career started several years ago!. It's been an amazing journey. I was introduce to music as a kid growing up. Had an amazing uncle who will always play most of the popular gospel songs, with reknown gospel artists like; Donmoen, Dalene Zschech, Women of Faith, Sinach, Frank Edwards, Isreal Houghton and more. This Initiation was the beginning of a dream I never thought will get me to where I am today. My formal introduction to music was when I started learning an Instrument(The drums). I worked with a wonderful friend (Nightengale), who taught me the drumz basics and more. I later worked with a friend(Fabrice) with whom I played with for about 2 years in church. I later develped more passion for music when I started learning the keyboard with amazing teachers like Ringwi Charles, Backbuin Kerry amd more. This was all in church and was an amazong experiece. Though it was difficult to be encouraged in church as most people don't understand the essence of mentoring and encourgaging instrumentalists, I willingly offered my service as act of worship to God. I later was introduced to HIPI Love Band. This was a music band that encouraged and notured me not just as an instrumentalists but as a christian. Thanks to them, I showed up on several of their programs. I later was introduced to several Ministers such as Min. Jay Praise, Min. Wanje, Min. Osvalin and more. Currently, I teach music on my YouTube channel, offer personal tutor and speedup classes for absolute beginners"
